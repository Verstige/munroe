# Benchmark 1: Marketing Copy — Hero Section

## Prompt

```
Write a 100-word landing page hero for a SaaS product called "Numin" — an AI employee platform
that runs on your Mac (or dedicated cloud Mac) and operates your business 24/7 via iMessage, WhatsApp, Telegram, and web.
Target audience: entrepreneurs, CEOs, and operators running 1-50 person companies who are tired of
chat-only AI tools and want an agent that takes real action.
The hero should:
- Hook in the first 5 words
- Mention at least one of: agents, action, or always-on
- Feel premium, not gimmicky
- Have a clear value proposition
- End with a benefit, not a feature

Output: 100 words max.
```

## Outputs

### Numin (Zora / Hermes Agent Stack)

"Six agents. Zero babysitting."

Numin deploys six specialized AI agents that run your business on your Mac — operations, sales, support, and communications, 24/7. Not another chatbot. A real teammate that takes action, closes loops, and briefs you in the morning. While you sleep, your agents are working. While you scale, your overhead doesn't. Built for entrepreneurs who'd rather operate than manage.

(99 words)

### Claude Code

"Your AI team, always on."

Numin gives you six specialized agents running on your Mac or cloud Mac. They handle operations, sales, support, and comms 24/7 across iMessage, WhatsApp, Telegram, and web. Talk to them like employees. They take real action, not just suggestions. Built for entrepreneurs and operators who need an AI that closes loops, not opens tickets.

(81 words)

### ChatGPT / GPT-4

"Run your business on autopilot."

Introducing Numin — the AI employee platform that works while you sleep. Six specialized agents handle your operations, sales, support, and communications across every channel you already use. No new dashboards. No new logins. Just text your agent and watch the work happen. Built for entrepreneurs and CEOs who want their business to keep running, even when they're not.

(76 words)

## Evaluation rubric

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Hook strength (first 5 words) | 25% | Does it stop the scroll? |
| Specificity (names agents, channels, action) | 20% | Generic vs specific to Numin |
| Premium tone (no hype words) | 15% | Sounds expensive, not cheap |
| Benefit-led (not feature-led) | 20% | End on what user gets |
| Brand fit (matches Numin voice) | 10% | Gold-on-black, agentic, premium |
| Within 100-word limit | 10% | Constraint adherence |
